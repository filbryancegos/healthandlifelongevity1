Shipping, Returns & Refunds

Shipping

The products we have are kept at fulfillment centers found in strategic areas across the United States and then, shipped from a locale nearby not long after you successfully place your order. This would explain why the address on your parcel may not be the same as the address on our website, and why shipping carriers may vary between shipments. Although products are usually delivered in a span of 2-3 days, it may at times be longer depending on your location in relation to the nearest fulfillment center.

At the moment, Health and Life Longevity transports products to areas within the United States and U.S. regions, including Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam, and the US Virgin Islands. In addition, Health and Life Longevity sends products to Australia, Canada and Mexico, and other international locations. The risk of loss and title for all products ordered on this website pass onto you when the stock is conveyed to the shipping carrier. If you need particular shipping and/or receiving directions, please convey this in the order notes during checkout. Health and Life Longevity will not be held accountable for any lost or stolen packages. Should your package be lost or stolen, please inform Health and Life Longevity at the soonest time at info@healthandlifelongevity.com so we can reach out to the carrier. Please get in touch with us if you would like to make orders that are more than 20 units.

Refunds

Once your return is received and checked, an email notifying you that we have received your returned item would be sent. We may opt to reject your refund if the item does not match the reason for return indicated in your original email.

If you are ratified, your refund will be handled and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or initial method of payment.


LATE OR MISSING REFUNDS


If the correction has not reflected on your account just yet, check with your bank first. Then get in touch with your credit card company, it may take a while before your refund is posted. The next step would be to contact your bank. At times there is a processing period before a refund is posted. If you’ve managed to do all of this and still, you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at info@healthandlifelongevity.com.

Returns

Unopened Items - You may send back unused and unopened items within 14 days of getting the item for a full refund. Items must be in the very same condition you got it. The original packaging must also be intact.

Broken or Damaged Items - You must begin a return request for items broken or broken during shipment within 7 days of getting the item. It must also be sent back in the original packaging.

Expired Items - In the rare circumstance that you do get an expired item, or an item that would expire before the ideal time frame indicated on the bottle, please take a picture of the bottom of the bottle/container and send it to info@healthandlifelongevity.com.


We will quickly send you a new item if your product(s) qualify.


Items Purchased with Loyalty Points - If you made use of loyalty points to buy your product, and we have accepted your refund, we would automatically remit these points back to your loyalty program. You must indicate that you used loyalty points in your refund email below.

We would only issue a refund to the credit card that was used at the time of checkout. Never send products back to any area listed on our website. Please be careful to follow the instructions below.

To initiate a return:

Email info@healthandlifelongevity.com with the following subject: Return Request for Order #[enter order #]

Write the following message: “I would like to return [enter item name] purchased on your website under order #[enter order #]. I am requesting a return because [enter reason for return].”

If your item qualifies for return, we would send you a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) form and a link to a shipping label. We would also inform you if your item is not suitable for return. Responses may possibly take anywhere between 24 to 48 hours.

Once you receive our email and your item is eligible for return, click on the shipping label link and print out the label.

Make sure the item(s) are in the original box and attach the shipping label to the box.

Drop the box off at your nearby postal office.
